White Water Rafting Adventures

At White Water Rafting Adventures, our mission is to provide safe, thrilling, and unforgettable rafting experiences that connect people with nature's raw power while fostering teamwork and adventure spirit.

History

Founded in 1998 by river enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Johnson, White Water Rafting Adventures began with just two rafts and a passion for sharing the thrill of river navigation. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's premier rafting companies.
