History

Founded in 1995 at 123 Main River Street, Kolob, California, Exciting Expeditions was born from a simple dream: to share the wild beauty of California's rivers with as many people as possible. What started as a small operation with three rafts and an old truck parked in front of our tiny shop in Kolob has become, thirty years later, a reference for rafting throughout California.

We began by exploring the rapids of the Kern River, before expanding our expeditions across the entire state, from the American River to the Tuolumne River gorges. Each season, we train new guides, improve our equipment, and discover new routes. But one thing has never changed: our commitment to providing safe, environmentally respectful, and unforgettable adventures, always from our historic headquarters in Kolob.
